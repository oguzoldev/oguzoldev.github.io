Hello World!

April 20, 2020,
1 min read

I’ve been wanting to write a blog for a long time, and here I am.

Initially, I had considered Jekyll for the blog, but I ended up choosing Hugo. I have an interest in the Go (Golang) language, so that’s why I opted for Hugo.

On my personal website, I’ll try to share the programs I use, my passion for coding, the technologies I utilize, and my continually evolving workspace. Additionally, I’ll be sharing what I create in my workshop.

Moreover, I want to assist those who are trying to learn computer science independently. Being a self-learner myself, I understand the challenges of entering the field on your own, and I would like to share the knowledge I’ve accumulated over all these years. If any of these resonate with you, feel free to follow me on social media.

Follow me on twitter: @o9uzdev. For more information about my professional journey, visit my LinkedIn profile.
